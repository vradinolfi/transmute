Transmu.te
A pretty Magic: The Gathering lookup tool
Transmu.te allows you to view detailed Oracle information on cards, as well as to-the-minute TCGplayer pricing, and competitive decklists utlizing those cards.
Transmu.te was made by Vincent Adinolfi utilizing the magic-api by Stephen Griffiths.
